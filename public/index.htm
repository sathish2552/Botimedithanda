About Our Village

Welcome to బోటి మీది తండా! Our village is rich in culture, tradition, and natural beauty. We take pride in our close-knit community and shared values.

Botimeedithanda is a small Village/hamlet in Narayanapur Mandal in Yadadrigiri Bhuvanagiri District of Telangana State, India. It comes under Janagam Panchayath. It belongs to Telangana region . It is located 46 KM towards west from District head quarters Nalgonda. Botimeedithanda Pin code is 508253 and postal head office is Narayanpur (Nalgonda). Botimeedithanda is surrounded by Choutuppal Mandal towards North , Munugode Mandal towards East , Manchal Mandal towards west , Chityala Mandal towards East . Bhongir , Hyderabad , Devarakonda , Jangaon are the near by Cities to Botimeedithanda. This Place is in the border of the Nalgonda District and Rangareddi District. Rangareddi District Manchal is west towards this place .
